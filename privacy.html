Privacy Policy — VibeSense

Effective date: July 8, 2026
Last updated: July 8, 2026

1. Who We Are

VibeSense is operated by VIBESENSE TECNOLOGIA LTDA ("we", "our", or "us"), based in Brazil. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use the VibeSense mobile app ("App").

Questions or requests? Contact us at admin@vibesense.live.

2. What Data We Collect

2.1 Data you provide directly

Data Why we collect it
Email address To create and identify your account (via Sign in with Apple or Sign in with Google)
Display name Provided by Apple/Google at sign-in; used to personalize your experience

2.2 Data collected automatically

Data Why we collect it
Precise GPS location To find bars and restaurants near you and check whether your city is supported
Favorites and venue interactions (hot-likes, views) To power your saved places and show popular venues
AI chat messages To answer your questions about nearby venues via our in-app assistant
Device identifier (Firebase UID) To maintain your session and link your data across devices

2.3 Data we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect your contacts, photos, microphone, or camera data.
  • We do not collect payment or financial information.
  • We do not build advertising profiles or sell your data to third parties.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data exclusively to provide and improve the App:

  • Venue discovery: Your location is sent to our servers to return nearby venues and their current busyness levels.
  • Account management: Your email and Firebase UID are used to authenticate you and keep your favorites synced.
  • AI assistant: Messages you send to the in-app chat are processed by our backend (which uses OpenAI's API) to generate venue suggestions. We do not store chat messages permanently.
  • Service improvement: Aggregated, anonymized usage patterns help us improve recommendations and expand to new cities.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the App's core function.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (LGPD)

Under Brazil's Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados (Law No. 13,709/2018), our legal bases for processing are:

Processing activity Legal basis (LGPD Art. 7)
Location data for venue discovery Execution of a contract / legitimate interest
Account creation (email, UID) Execution of a contract
Favorites and engagement data Execution of a contract / legitimate interest
AI chat messages Execution of a contract / legitimate interest

5. Who We Share Your Data With

We share data only with the service providers strictly necessary to operate the App:

Provider Purpose Data shared
Google Firebase Authentication Email, display name, UID
Apple Sign in with Apple Email (or private relay), name
Amazon Web Services (AWS) Cloud hosting of our API and database (Brazil region where available) All app data
OpenAI AI chat responses Chat message content (no name or email sent)

All providers are bound by data processing agreements and may not use your data for their own purposes.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or data brokers.

6. Data Retention

Data Retention period
Account data (email, UID) Until you delete your account
Location data Not stored — used in real time per request only
Favorites / engagement Until you delete the item or your account
AI chat messages Not stored persistently — processed in real time
Aggregated analytics Indefinitely (no personal identifiers)

7. Your Rights (LGPD)

As a user in Brazil, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Delete your data and account at any time.
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured format.
  • Withdraw consent at any time (where processing is consent-based).
  • Know which third parties we share your data with.
  • Lodge a complaint with Brazil's data protection authority (ANPD) at www.gov.br/anpd.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at admin@vibesense.live. We will respond within 15 business days.

Deleting your account: You can request account deletion by emailing us. All personal data will be removed within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

8. Children's Privacy

VibeSense is not directed at children under 13 (or under 18 where applicable local law requires). We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal data, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS) for all API calls.
  • Firebase Authentication tokens for session security.
  • Access-controlled cloud infrastructure (AWS VPC, private subnets).

No system is 100% secure. If you suspect unauthorized access to your account, contact us immediately.

10. Location Data

Precise location is requested only while using the App and only to serve venue results. We do not store your location history. You can revoke location permission at any time in your device settings — the App will continue to work with degraded accuracy (manual city selection).

11. Third-Party Sign-In

When you sign in with Apple or Google, those providers may collect data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Apple Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/privacy/
  • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

We receive only the minimum data needed to create your account (email and a unique identifier).

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in the App or applicable law. We will notify you of material changes via the App or by email. Continued use of the App after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
